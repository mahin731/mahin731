## 🚀 About Me

I'm an **Aerospace Engineer** with a specialisation in **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning** — where the sky isn't the limit, it's just the beginning. With 1 year of hands-on experience, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and real-world aerospace systems, building autonomous platforms that think, adapt, and fly.

- 🛸 **Aerospace meets AI**: I design and deploy intelligent systems for UAVs, autonomous navigation, and flight control
- 📹 **Content Creator** on [YouTube](https://youtube.com/@mahinnandipa3049), [Medium](https://medium.com/@mahin.nandipa), and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/ecstamyn.cosride) — sharing knowledge on aerospace & AI
- 🧠 **Currently obsessed with**: Reinforcement Learning for autonomous flight & Swarm Intelligence
- ⚡ **Fun fact**: I once debugged a drone mid-flight (mentally) — and it worked 🎯
- 🌍 **Mission**: Democratise autonomous aerospace technology through open-source and education
